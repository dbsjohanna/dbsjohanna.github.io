    IMAGINE Institut, Paris, France

    Gustave Roussy Institut, Villejuif, France
    Supervisor: Dr. Pierre Savagner

    Project: The epithelial-mesenchymal transition during breast cancer.
    Study of the activation of genes associated with the epithelial-mesenchymal transition using immunocolocalization, fluorescence microscopy and slide scanning.

    Rouen, France
