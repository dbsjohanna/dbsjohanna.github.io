Research Experiences

  • Ph.D. Fellow, Inserm U959 - Immunology, Immunopathology, Immunotherapy
    2021 - on going
    Sorbonne-Université, Paris, France

    Supervisors: Dr. Michelle Rosenzwajg and Dr. Nicolas Tchitchek
    Project: Characterization of the impact of low-dose interleukin-2 treatment on immune cell dynamics in healthy volunteers using different omics (immunophenotyping, immunoproteomic, transcriptomic).

    Contributions: Cytometric data analysis using supervised (compensation and gating) and unsupervised methods.
    Test of several pipeline for cytometric unsupervised analysis.
    Luminex protocol to quantify 75 cytokines, chemokines and soluble receptors in serum and data analysis.
    RNA extraction and sequencing with transcriptomic data analysis.
    Data integration.
    Collaboration in other laboratory projects.
    Achievements: Several submitted articles.

  • M2 Intern, Inserm U959 - Immunology, Immunopathology, Immunotherapy
    03/2021 - 09/2021
    Sorbonne-Université, Paris, France

    Supervisor: Dr. Nicolas Tchitchek
    Project: Analysis of cytometric data from clinical trials using supervised and unsupervised methods to redefine the noslogy of autoimmune diseases.

    Contributions: Cytometric data analysis using unsupervised methods.
    Batch correction
    Achievements: Article published in Ann Rheum Dis (2024).

  • M2 Intern, Inserm U1163 - Lymphocyte activation and susceptibility to EBV
    01/2020 - 06/2020
    IMAGINE Institut, Paris, France

    Supervisor: Dr. Emmanuel Martin
    Project: Analysis of CTPS1 promoter by inhibition of its transcription factors (siRNA, shRNA, CRISPR) and detection of mutations by genomic DNA sequencing.


  • M1 Intern, Inserm U1186 - Integrative immunology of tumors
    04/2019 - 06/2019
    Gustave Roussy Institut, Villejuif, France

    Supervisor: Dr. Pierre Savagner
    Project: Study of the activation of genes associated with epithelial-mesenchymal transition in the context of breast cancer using immunolocalization, fluorescence microscopy and slide scanning.


  • L3 Intern, Inserm U1234 - Pathophysiology, Autoimmunity, Neuromuscular diseases and Regenerative therapies
    04/2018 - 06/2018
    Rouen, France

    Supervisor: Dr. Laurent Drouot
    Project: Study of the involvement of type I interferons in myositis using PCR, Luminex and ELISA analysis.
